Organisation

Assistant Manager - Maintenance (MECH)
I am working as an assistant manager in mechanical maintenance team of bakeoven. In bakeoven, anodes are baked that are being used in potline for pure aluminium production. Here I am looking after furnace and its fuel storage and circulation system as an area engineer. The furnace area equipments mainly consist of screw pumps, gear pumps, blowing fans, injectors, heaters, PRVs and various types of couplings, where I need to ensure 100% availability of operating equipment, equipment spares and also need to minimise the energy consumption for baking the anode in furnace in every means. Team work and leadership is very important to work in one of the biggest metal producers like vedanta where I experience both here. Working in this industry I have involved in many kaizen activities, QC projects and six sigma projects.
